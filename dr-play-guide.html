Play Guide

Includes information on how mechanics work, features, some event explanations, and some translated work. [Image heavy.]

Click the label to view.

[JPN] [ENG]

Battle

※ If you die during battle, you can resurrect by consuming charms 「おまもり」. Some quests will not allow you to use them.

Main Quest / Main Mission

Continuing the main quest (story), tap the button in the top right of the Home screen, below the Menu button. However, if there are main missions to complete, it will replace that button. Once you complete those missions, the quest will open up again.

※ Theater mode will be available in future updates.
※ Currently, up to quest 20 is available. Story scenes occur at quests 7, 10, 14, and 20.

Free Battle

This is an endless mode where enemies will continue to appear. Use it to obtain BP, fill the card album, and complete missions. Difficulty will be increased as story progresses.

※ Max BP for one free battle is 999 999 999.
※ When the date switches at 04:00 the following day, you will be automatically kicked to the home screen.
※ You can quit Free Battle by tapping the button in the top left of the screen. Despite quitting part way, you will still receive the accumulated BP and enemy count.

Cards

Cards can be obtained via jewel or ticket draws.

There are 6 "colours” available: Light red, light green, light blue, dark red, dark green, and dark blue. Up to 5 cards of each colour (total 30) can be included in your card deck.

The same cards can not be kept separate. When you obtain a multiple of one card, they will be automatically combined and the strength of it will be increased.

※ Some cards will not have their valued increased. For these, the passive + value will be higher than others.

The max number of cards that can be combined is 10 and anything above can be converted to either BP or SP (shop points).

※ In the options menu, you can decide to have the game automatically choose either BP or SP if you so desire. The amount of points is dependent on the card.
※ For cards that don’t combine normally, the amount of points converted will be increased compared to normal cards.

Level Up

From the status screen (bottom left button at Home), leveling up Xehanort requires the use of BP.

Getting BP

As BP can be obtained via battles and missions, it will also accumulate as time passes. This BP can be obtained by pressing the BP icon at the top of the Home screen. This BP can reach 50 000 before it must be claimed.

Important Notes

Closing (and reopening) the app while playing:

  • Events: It will restart from the beginning and will consume revive charms if any were used.
  • Main quest: You will return to the Home screen and will consume revive charms if any were used.
  • Free battle: You will return to the Home screen, keep the earned BP, but lose revive charms if used.

Updates while playing:

  • Events: Same as if you closed and reopened the app.
  • Free battle: Same as if you closed and reopened the app.

Added: version 4.4.0/5.0.0 [x]

Home Screen

(1) Free Battle

Endlessly fight enemies for BP, materials, and to fill your enemy album.

Select the world and difficulty level, then tap the "Free Battle" button.

Battle can be conducted automatically by pressing the "Auto" button.

(2) Main Quest

Play through the entire story.

(3) How to play

Get a rundown on gameplay.

(4) Craft

Create accessories with materials to enhance your stats.

They can be equipped in the "Status" menu.

(5) Status

Level up and change Xehanort's equipment here.

"Change Deck" allows you to equip cards for use in battle.

You can let the game choose for you by tapping the "Optimize" button.

(6) Album

Fill up the Enemy Album and Card Album.

Filling these will give you passive stat enhancements.

(7) Theatre

Previously viewed scenes can be rewatched here.

(8) Shop

Spend BP for cards and items.
