Added: version 1.4.0 [x]

A new technique that is used once per quest by tapping the icon in the top right.

  • Burst is a function that is available once a medal achieves guilt.
  • In quests and raid boss battles, it can only be used once.
  • The level of Burst can increase.

Leveling Burst

Burst level can be increased by the following:

  • Achieving any percentage of guilt on a medal.
  • Achieving max guilt on a medal.

※ Burst level will not increase if you max guilt another of the same medal.
※ Status bonus and non-Status Bonus medals still count as the same medal.

Other

Medals that have been guilted or have max guilt before the update will have burst already applied.

Level of Burst on a particular medal can be checked in the medal album.

Added: version 2.0.0 [x]

Play with up to 6 people at once!

About

Defeat Heartless with other players-- either from your party or union.
Get Cross Coins by clearing the quest and through other means.

※ Cross Coins can only be used during events.
※ Access Union Cross from the Quest menu. You must have cleared story quest 81.

How Quests Work

The main objective is to clear the quest. Additionally, Time Missions may appear.

The stage and missions are randomly selected when you enter. So be prepared for all situations.

Matching

The Union Cross start screen is similar to the normal quest screen.

Immediate Start 「すぐにスタート」 will gather yourself and other union members.
Party Start 「パーティーでスタート」 is where you can join your other party members.

On the quest result screen, play with the same members 「同じメンバーと再プレイ」 will allow you to continue playing with the same people.

Resurrection

When you lose all your HP, you become inactive, and a count and gauge is displayed.

Party members can revive you by tapping the icon as many times as the count indicates, assuming this has been done before the gauge disappears.
Members that tap will use one medal slot for each tap.
Every time you reach 0 HP, the count increases.

However, if the gauge disappears without being resurrected, you can use jewels to restore your HP, or you can retire.

Comments

During quests, you can send messages to other members by tapping the icon in the bottom right and releasing on the comment you want to use.

On the Union Cross start screen, you can edit comments through the bottom left button: Comment Edit 「コメント編集」.

Other

By pressing the comment button and closing it, icons of your party member locations are displayed.

Players that have played together can check the history from the main screen by tapping 「一緒に遊んだプレイヤー」. So when you want to invite the same people, search there.

Added: version 2.0.0 [x] [x]

About

By synthesizing the following, an Awakening Ability is added to a medal:

  • the same medal of any rarity
  • a designated awakening medal

Awakening Ability is an effect that always activates.

  • Mickey & Broom Servant medals cannot have Awakening Abilities.
  • Even if multiple of the same medals are synthesized at the same time, only one Awakening Ability will occur.

There are multiple types of Awakening Abilities and are randomly decided each synthesis.
Depending on the medal, certain number of slots that can be awakened are fixed.

When you try to create a new Awakening Ability with the medal at full slots, you can choose to overwrite or not. If you don’t, it will fuse as normal.

List of Awakening Abilities

Effective on the whole deck:

  • Max ability gauge +2 「アビリティゲージ最大値＋2」
  • Max HP +800 「HP最大値+800」
  • Poison resistance 20% 「毒耐性20%」
  • Paralysis resistance 20% 「麻痺耐性20%」
  • Sleep tolerance 20% 「睡眠耐性20%」

Effective on the medal with the awakening:

  • Ground enemy defence -60% 「地上エネミーの防御力-60%」
  • Air enemy defence -60% 「空中エネミーの防御力-60%」
  • Raid boss battle damage +40% 「レイド戦でダメージ+40%」
  • Additional attack +40% 「追加攻撃 威力40%」: attack again with the same medal. 40% of damage dealt.
  • Attack power +1000 「攻撃力+1000」
  • Defence power +2000 「防御力+2000」

Even if a medal has Additional Attack and enemy defences more than once, only one will take effect. The others can be stacked.

Exclusive Awakening Medals

Combining the Awakening Medal with the original medal has the following effects:

  • Adds an Awakening Ability
  • Increases the Ability level by 1

Notes:

  • Awakening Medals are only for synthesizing, it does not act like the original medal.
  • Its cost is at 999 so it can't be equipped to a deck. However, it can be equipped to the pet slot or used as a partner medal, but isn't suitable for attacking. (The Awakening Medal will have the appearance as its original medal except in greyscale.)
  • It counts for 15 pet points.
  • It will always be pulled with Status Bonus, but there will be no change to the medal.
  • In the medal album, the original medal and Awakening Medal are treated the same.
  • When the original medal is a score bonus medal in a High Score event, the Awakening Medal will be too.

Added: version 2.3.0 [x]

The pet system allows you to adventure with your pet!
※ Story quest 14 must be completed.

About

Pets are equipped with medals and will attack with the medal that is used as your sixth slot.
There are also special skills that can be invoked during battle.

You can also name your pet, change its appearance, and power it up.

Equipping your pet with a medal to attack

Press the Pet 「ペット」 button on the equipment screen to drag a medal to its slot.
The equipped medal is used as a sixth slot during battle.

Appearance & Strengthening

Tap on your pet by your avatar in the upper left corner of the screen to open the pet main screen where you can change its name, appearance, power-up, and view special skills.

Create your own pet!

By pressing the "Co-ord" 「コーデ」 button, you can change your pet’s appearance!
Mix and match parts to create your unique pet.

Strengthening pet rank

By pressing the "power-up" 「強化」 button, you can set medals (separate from medals used in battle) to enhance pet rank.

The pet rank is determined by the total points of the six medals.
(A) When the rank increases, the pet medal slot multiplier will increase. The pet may also learn new skills.

Medals have points that strengthen the pet’s rank which vary on the medal. Medals with more awakening ability slots have increased points.
(B) When the "leave it to you" 「おまかせ」 button is pressed, the medals with the most points will be automatically set.

Skills will be activated under certain conditions during battle to help the player.
Skills and their details can be checked via the "Special Skills List" 「特技一覧」 button on the pet main screen.

※ Medals used for increasing pet rank will not be consumed, so they can be changed freely.
※ Medals equipped in Keyblades can also be used for leveling.

Added: version 2.4.0 [x]

Boost items can strengthen medals for a limited time.

Boosts can be obtained through rewards from events and other means. It will automatically take effect when you receive it.
※ The end date and time is fixed.

You can check your Boost inventory via the "Boost" 「ブースト」 button on your profile screen.

Medals that are affected by the Boost will have info displayed on its medal detail screen.

※ Boosts with the same attribute can be stacked.
※ Copy abilities: The copy medal's attribute does not matter. Boosts of the copied medal's attribute will be applied. Example: HD Namine copying Dual Wield Roxas [EX] will only be affected by Power Boosts, not Magic Boosts. Same applies for Upright and Reverse Boosts.

Added: version 2.6.0 [x]

Set 7* medals to increase Keyblade slot multipliers!

Subslots have been added to each Keyblade.

By setting 7* medals into subslots the multiplier of the Keyblade's slots will be increased.
※ For pets, the pet slot multiplier is affected by the select Keyblade's subslots.

The value increase depends on the medal's Guilt tier.

Setting the Medals

Subslot medals can be set by opening the Subslot menu from the Keyblade equipment screen.

Only 7* medals can be set in subslots.
Additionally, only medals that match the attribute and direction can fit.

To benefit from subslots, you must place medals in order.

Added: version 2.6.0 [x]

About

VS Player (PvP Mode) is Coliseum content where players fight each other using multiple Keyblades.

HP is infinite, so whoever deals the most damage to the opponent wins.

The rankings are held weekly from Tuesday to Monday. Usable Keyblades are fixed for each week as well.

The number of times you can challenge per day are fixed, and if you purchase jewels, the number of times is increased.
※ This is valid until 23:59 Sunday of the week of your purchase.

Battle Rules

Three Keyblades are used; each turn is alternated between the players. The player who deals the most damage wins, with the best of 2 out of 3.

※ Whoever attacks first each turn is alternated.
※ Buffs and debuffs that last multiple turns are carried over appropriately.

If you win against your opponent, you can switch ranking with them.
Example: You (rank 1000) win against your opponent (rank 500).
=> You (rank 500) / opponent (rank 1000).

※ If you win against an opponent with a lower rank than you, the rankings will not change.
※ When switching to the VS Player main screen, your new rank will be updated and you can face a new set of players.

There is a 9 minutes 20 seconds timer (5 minute for single Keyblade weeks), where if time runs out, it will be treated as a defeat.
※ In this case, no changes will be made to the rankings.

Other Rules

You and your opponent are considered "no attribute" so you are not weak to any attribute.
※ Excludes abilities with "super effective damage".

Status effects work but the length differs from normal battle.

The opponent is treated as a ground enemy.

Keyblade Equipment

On the VS Player Keyblade preparation screen, the Keyblade order and decks can be changed.

  • By dragging the Keyblade you can change the order you use them.
  • Unlike normal decks, medals cannot be placed in multiple decks.
  • Keyblade order and medal placement is key!

Point Rewards

  • You can earn points through battles for rewards.
  • Points can be obtained in draws (battle results, not banners) and defeats, but you get more for winning.
  • Points are reset when the ranking period ends.

Other

Changed some daily Missions to VS Player.
※ Conditions will not be met if you retire in the middle of a VS Players match.

Tips

Notice [x]

1. Prevent the opponent's attacks with Abnormal Status Skills

  • By inflicting sleep or paralyze on your opponent, it can cause them to skip their first few medals. Since the first medals are typically buff or debuff medals, this can greatly reduce their damage.
  • Beware of the pet's one use skill "Esuna" that can heal the opponent's abnormal status. Make sure to equip abnormal status skills on multiple Keyblades.
  • ※ The pet skill "Paralyze Up" can help turn the tide in your favour.
  • ※ If you inflict Sleep on your opponent, attacking them after will wake them up.

2. Reduce the opponent's damage with Guard Up Skills

  • A simple way to decrease your opponent's damage is to equip Guard Up skills to your medals.
  • Bring Guard Up 100% skills to ensure safety. If it's not a 100% skill, equip accessories with Skill Luck.
  • ※ Note that the ability "Guard Break", that will be discussed later, can make the skills ineffective.

3. Activate Super Burst effectively

  • Using Super Bursts that increase Guilt will help increase damage significantly.
  • Since the Guilt increase does not stack, use the highest one first then fire off the rest.
  • ※ If Auto is on, Super Bursts will not automatically activate.

4. Destroy the opponent's defences with Guard Up Break

  • Guard Break is an ability that has a chance for each hit to break the opponent's Guard Up skill. Breaking the Guard Up skill will make it ineffective for that hit.
  • Because it's an ability (and not a skill), copying it with Copy medals will also trigger it. Even if the Copy medal has a low Guilt value, it can still do a ton of damage.

5. Set up Defence Decks to maintain your ranking

  • When someone chooses to attack you, the person who goes first is switched, so take this into consideration when creating your decks. If you want to skip your opponent's attacks, equip abnormal status skills to your first Keyblade.

Super Burst

Super Burst can be activated any time during your turn and only be used once per quest. However, you must have that medal equipped to use it.

※ If you equip multiple medals with Super Burst, you can use them all (even if it's the same medal name).

The damage of Super Burst grows stronger according to the power of the medal with that particular Super Burst.

※ Slot multiplier and buffs are applied to its damage. Guilt percentage and skills do not affect it.

In VS Player (PVP), activation during defence battles (when someone challenges you) varies depending on the medal. This is noted in the medal detail screen under Awakenings.

Platinum Tickets & Dual Meow Wow

Get five [Guilt 7 - 9] Meow Wow SP & NM medals from this banner.

SB Evolution & Enhancement

Certain medals can be "evolved" to either add a Super Burst effect or increase its effect using the new medal.

Target SB medals will be evolved to SB+, while target medals will have an SB effect unlocked.

SB medals will be reset to level 1 (but not its stats) so they can be powered up even farther. With this, Chips and Dales can increase the extra strength and defence to +3000.

※ To evolve the medal, the 5 Meow Wow medals must match its Guilt tier.
※ Only medals with SB already attached can be evolved to SB+.

※ Targeted medals are indicated in the medal notice.
※ SB medals that are evolved will have their strength and defence in yellow font. To distinguish the difference from Status Bonus medals, the Cost stat for Status Bonus medals will also be in yellow font.

※ Platinum tickets are mainly from PVP.
※ Meow Wow SP & NM medals may be available from elsewhere including PVE Coliseum.

Event Details

  • Score can be increased by attacking and defeating enemies. The final score is determined when the target enemy is destroyed or when its HP is depleted.
  • Continues cannot be used, so when your HP reaches 0, the quest is over.
  • Score gained is increased with an enemy's level and strength. So even when against the same enemy, the more damage dealt, the more points you get.

Incorporating Bonus Medals into Decks

  • When a Bonus Medal is equipped to the deck, the score bonus is added when you attack and defeat the enemy.
    You can check the Bonus Medals in the event.

※ You can check the Bonus Medals in the event.

Aiming for High Damage

  • The higher the damage when destroying the enemy, the score added is increased.
  • When fighting enemies, use abilities with high damage and a low hit count.

Let's get a damage bonus with strong abilities!

  • When attacking an enemy with high power, a "Damage Bonus" is gained by defeating an enemy, the score is increased by up to 40%.
    The value is calculated depending on the strongest attack used at the time.
  • "Damage Bonus" is based on the strongest attack until the enemy is defeated. The greater the power, the higher the increase.
    The number of times the strongest attack is used is not calculated, so the Damage Bonus is not increased even if that attack is used multiple times against the same enemy.
    So use strong abilities against as many enemies as you can.
  • The strongest attack is determined by the following rules:
  • Calculating the Damage Bonus, as the enemy’s defence has a fixed value
  • ...When abilities are used against enemies with high defence and level, the Damage Bonus is not lowered unlike other enemies.
    As high level enemies already have a high amount of score, you can expect a large Damage Bonus.
    So use abilities often.
  • Medal strength, Keyblade multiplier, buffs, and skills all increase the Damage Bonus
  • ...A higher Damage Bonus can be achieved by overlapping attacks for medals with high strength, abilities with a high multiplier, buff effects, and skill triggers.

※ Note that using a "Skip Ticket" will not give a score.

Added: version 3.4.0 [x]

Certain passive "buffs" will be applied to yourself depending on your Lux level or Keyblade level.

Based off Lux level:

  • From level 350, stats (strength, defence, HP, ability gauges) are affected.
  • By level 800, strength +3000 (applies to all medals), defence +3000, HP +5000, AG +3 (all Keyblades).
  • Passives are added or upgraded every 5 levels from level 350 - 450, every 2 levels from level 450 - 550, and every 1 level from 550+.

Based off Keyblade level:

  • From [Keyblade] +35, passives affect the strength of Upright/Power, Upright/Speed, Upright/Magic, Reverse/Power, Reverse/Speed, Reverse/Magic, Power, Speed, Magic, Upright, and Reverse.
  • The passive will only affect the specific medal attribute that matches.
  • Examples:
    • Upright/Power medal strength +50 => Upright/Power medals
    • Power medal strength +100 => ignores Upright or Reverse; Power medals

Passives can be checked in the 「パッシブ効果」 "Passive Effect" button on your avatar profile.

Added: January 12, 2017 [x]

Get medals "Puppy No. 1-99", "Pongo", and "Perdita" from story quests. Defeating enemies and opening chests have a chance of dropping these adorable doggos.

By arranging the Puppy medals accordingly will be key to evolving them.

Example: "Puppy 1" to "Puppy 6" can evolve into "Puppies No. 1 - 6", and so on. Also works with the "Puppies No. # - #" medals, i.e., "Puppies No. 1 - 6" to "Puppies No. 31 - 36" can evolve into "Puppies No. 1 - 36".

Burst Level

Ultimately, you can evolve them into "101 Dalmatians". When Guilted, your Burst Level will increase by the usual 1. When reaching max Guilt, your Burst Level will increase by 100, totaling to 101.

"Pongo" and "Perdita" can also increase the ability level of "101 Dalmatians".

Note on Awakening Abilities: Unlike increasing the ability level of the 101 Dalmatians medal with Pongo and Perdita, the two will not reroll the Awakening Ability on 101 Dalmatians.
