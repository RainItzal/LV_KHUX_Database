December 7, 2015 [x]

Quest Additions and Adjustments

Increased the amount of Huey, Dewey, and Louie medals dropped from the super growth medal quest.

Adamant Challenges

The level limit of each Keyblade has been increased to +21.

In the adamant challenge quests, get adamant materials for evolving your Keyblades. They rotate daily. Adamants can be used on any applicable Keyblade.

Upright and Reverse Medal Slots

In addition to matching attributes, matching the direction can further increase medal power. Upright and Reverse slots begin appearing from +21.

"Strength Bangle" Releases the 6th Stage of Abilities

By continuing through the main story, you can receive the Strength Bangle. The 6th stage can be rolled infinitely. It may be higher at random. However, it will never decrease.

The higher the guilt “tier”, the higher the strengthening rate (%).
