December 7, 2015 [x]

Quest Additions and Adjustments

Increased the amount of Huey, Dewey, and Louie medals dropped from the super growth medal quest.

Adamant Challenges

The level limit of each Keyblade has been increased to +21.

In the adamant challenge quests, get adamant materials for evolving your Keyblades. They rotate daily. Adamants can be used on any applicable Keyblade.

Upright and Reverse Medal Slots

In addition to matching attributes, matching the direction can further increase medal power. Upright and Reverse slots begin appearing from +21.

"Strength Bangle" Releases the 6th Stage of Abilities

By continuing through the main story, you can receive the Strength Bangle. The 6th stage can be rolled infinitely. It may be higher at random. However, it will never decrease.

The higher the guilt “tier”, the higher the strengthening rate (%).

August 4, 2016 [x]

Additions and Adjustments

Added Luck values to certain avatar parts.

You can check the active Luck values by tapping the Luck Confirmation 「ラック確認」 button on the avatar customization screen.

The Luck effects are as follows:

  • スキルラック: skill luck (skill activation is increased)
  • クリティカルラック: critical luck (critical hit activation is increased)
  • レアエネミーラック: rare enemy luck (chance of rare enemies appearing is increased)
  • ドロップラック: drop luck (rare item drop rate is increased)
  • レイド出現ラック: normal raid boss appearance luck (chance of a normal raid boss appearing is increased)
  • レイド亜種出現ラック: rare raid boss appearance luck (chance of a rare raid boss appearing is increased)

Once a day, players can now increase Lux for the entire party for 60 minutes by 5%.

On the Home screen, tap the Lux Up 「LUXアップ」 button to activate it.

The effect stacks with all members.

※ This can be done once per day, resetting at midnight.

Added multiple Keyblade decks.

※ The function is available once any Keyblade reaches +20.

When returning to the Home screen, the BGM will not restart from the beginning and instead continue from where it left off.

When either dying (without using continues) or retiring a quest, ability gauge is reset to before entering.

UI Changes

  • Synthesis, evolution, and Moogle medals are stackable to a max of 99, except Scrooge medals.
  • Party menus have been adjusted.
  • Character profiles have been updated to include Coliseum info.
  • The BGM will play when viewing a medal with it. The normal music will resume when you exit the medal.
  • For banners with a mercy, it will indicate how many more draws you need for it.
  • In battle, the de/buff icons will appear appropriately.

September 19, 2019 [x]

Moogle Shop

  • Items can be bought with jewels.
  • Some items can be purchased for a limited time, or via weekly VIP purchase.

Passives

  • Based off Lux level:
    • From level 350, stats (strength, defence, HP, ability gauges) are affected.
    • By level 800, strength +3000 (applies to all medals), defence +3000, HP +5000, AG +3 (all Keyblades).
    • Passives are added or upgraded every 5 levels from level 350 - 450, every 2 levels from level 450 - 550, and every 1 level from 550+.
  • Based off Keyblade level:
    • From [Keyblade] +35, passives affect the strength of Upright/Power, Upright/Speed, Upright/Magic, Reverse/Power, Reverse/Speed, Reverse/Magic, Power, Speed, Magic, Upright, and Reverse.
    • The passive will only affect the specific medal attribute that matches.
    • Examples:
      • Upright/Power medal strength +50 => Upright/Power medals
      • Power medal strength +100 => ignores Upright or Reverse; Power medals
  • Passives can be checked in the 「パッシブ効果」 "Passive Effect" button on your avatar profile.

Condensed Medal Fusing
