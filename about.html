About

Luxen Vulpies is a party that originated in the Japanese version of Kingdom Hearts Union χ[Cross], formerly Kingdom Hearts Unchained χ[Chi].

This site serves as a database for all things KHUχ. Info is based on the Japanese version as this is the version we focus on. There are several places to look for the English version's info.


The project is run by Rinax, who is also the creator and admin of the Luxen Vulpies Tumblr blog.

Heya! If you came here from our Tumblr blog (or checked it out after you got here), you might be wondering, "why bother with creating your own website when you already have the Tumblr itself and all those spreadsheets and documents?" Well, to be honest, mostly boredom (and a little bit of "because I want to practice HTML again.").

The actual reason is that I wanted to include images and other visuals to our spreadsheets (I'm a visual and hands-on learner), but it felt cumbersome that way (avatar part images were included, but I'm talking more in a general sense). I then realized that a website would be the best way to do just that! However, I'm not currently willing to purchase a domain or servers, as this is mostly for fun, so I uploaded everything to GitHub. It works well, so I'm content with that.

In any case, I hope you enjoy my work here and find it helpful in some way! Thanks for checking it out, too!
