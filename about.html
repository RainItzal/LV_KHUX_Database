About

Luxen Vulpies is a party that originated in the Japanese version of Kingdom Hearts Union χ[Cross], formerly Kingdom Hearts Unchained χ[Chi], and have expanded to the International version.

This site serves as a database for all things KHUχ. Info is based on the Japanese version as this is the version we focus on.
